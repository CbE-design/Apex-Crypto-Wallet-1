Since the wallet is non-custodial (meaning the user holds the keys), you cannot directly "touch" their money. Instead, you must act as the orchestrator between the blockchain and regulated financial on/off-ramps.As of March 2026, South Africa has implemented the Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework (CARF), making compliance-by-design essential for any local or international operation.1.
